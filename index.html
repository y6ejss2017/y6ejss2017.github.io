Welcome to our very own internet Hub

This site is a messageboard open to all students in Year 6 at Eagle Junction SS. It was created by Mac Graham.

Instructions

To use this site, scroll down to the messageboard link of your choice and click on it (We will add different boards as the year goes on). This is will bring you to the site of your desired messageboard. In the input field where it says "from", click the arrow nect to it and this will bring up a sign up dialogue. Fill out the form fields using your school email. You will then be asked to verify this. Search MIS in google and click on the top hit. You will then be asked to sign in with your school username and password. Once signed in, go to oulook and click the verification email. Then click the verify email link and you are all set.
